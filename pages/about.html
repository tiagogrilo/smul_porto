This website was created by Joel Vaz and Tiago Grilo for the course of SMUL in the Faculty of Engineering of the University of Porto. The beautiful architecture, the inspiring art and delicious cuisine are a few of our favorite things about the city we live in and that's why we decided to create a responsive, modern-looking website that includes text, images, video, animations, sound and graphics, i.e. a multimedia application, to show the world about the best of Porto.
