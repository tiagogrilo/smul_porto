Sunflowers

Sunflowers are a psych punk, noise pop band from Porto. With the perfect amounts of confused riffs, slurred lines and feedback insanity, the Portuguese outfit's popularity has grown in recent years. In over 150 shows, they’ve shared the stage with artists such as Oh Sees, Moon Duo, Black Lips, Boogarins, La Femme, The Blind Shake and Tomorrow’s Tulips.
