José Mário Branco, born in Porto, is a singer/songwriter. A representative of Música de Intervenção, he started his career during Salazar's dictatorship, so he had to move to France. As a composer and producer he worked with José Afonso, Sérgio Godinho, Luís Represas, Fausto and Camané, among others. He also writes music for theatre, cinema and television.
